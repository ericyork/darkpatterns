The Ploys of Cunning

A Visual Gallery of Deceptive Techniques

Disguise

Patterns that use total disguise to induce users to take harmful actions.

Camouflage

Patterns where affordances are muted so that links blend into their surroundings.

Misdirection

Patterns where users are encouraged to look at one thing at the expense of another.

Intimidation

Patterns in which the sheer mental effort involved in an action discourages users from attempting it.

Disorientation

Patterns that rely on ambiguous affordances and misleading microcopy.

Mind F*ckery

Patterns that require users to adopt compromising positions and identities.
